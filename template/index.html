Predicting Breast Cancer

According to the world health organization (WHO) Breast cancer is the most frequent cancer among women, impacting 2.1 million women each year, and also causes the greatest number of cancer-related deaths among women. Using this application, you can check if your cancer is malignant or benign. If the cancer is benign then you only need to take some preventions. And if it's malignant then you should see a doctor.
